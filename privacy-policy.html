Privacy Policy - Sit-to-Stand Test

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Sit-to-Stand Test mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") created by Sanchit Aggarwal (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS" and is available on Android and iOS platforms.

1. Overview

The Sit-to-Stand Test Application is designed to help users measure and track their lower limb strength through standardized five-repetition sit-to-stand tests. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Free vs. Pro Features

The Application offers both a free version and a Pro version (one-time purchase of $1.99 USD):

Free Version:

  • Limited to one test per day
  • Displays banner and interstitial advertisements
  • Basic test results and timing data
  • AdMob collects device and usage data for personalized advertising

Pro Version:

  • Unlimited daily tests
  • No advertisements
  • User profile management for tracking multiple individuals
  • Detailed per-repetition analytics and smoothness scores
  • Age-based normative comparison data
  • Export features (PDF reports and CSV data files)
Important: When you upgrade to Pro, advertising services are immediately disabled and ad-related data collection stops. Your purchase is tied to your Google Play or App Store account and automatically restores when you reinstall the application on any device using the same account.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored ONLY on your device and is NEVER transmitted to our servers:

  • Test Results: Completion times, repetition counts, timestamps
  • Profile Information (Pro users): Name, age, optional medical condition notes
  • Historical Data: All past test results and progress tracking
  • User Preferences: App settings and configurations

All local data is stored using encrypted database technology (Room Database) and remains under your complete control.

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

The Application collects limited technical information to function properly:

  • Device Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Operating system type and version
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Application usage statistics (pages visited, features used)
  • Time and date of app access
  • App version and crash reports

The Application does NOT collect precise location data.

3.3 Sensor Data

The Application requires access to your device's accelerometer sensor to:

  • Detect sit-to-stand movements during tests
  • Count repetitions automatically
  • Calculate movement smoothness (Pro feature)

This sensor data is processed in real-time on your device only and is not transmitted externally. Only the resulting test scores are saved locally.
